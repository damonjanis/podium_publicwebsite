Features

List Building

Build your list of prospective investors. Podium automates list building so it's effortless and pleasant.

Easily communicate with your contact list sharing information and preparing for fundraising.

Fundraising

Track and manage every step of the process. The invitations, soft commitments, subscription agreements, money received, and closing.

Podium handles all the communications and documents.

Deal Management

Manage your investor roster. Automatically calculate dividends and send communications to your investors.

Track all documentation like tax statements and investment updates.

Passive Portal

Passive Investors have a secure portal to all their deals. See and respond to investment offers from Leads.

View and track investments and distributions. Access all the important documents for each investment.
